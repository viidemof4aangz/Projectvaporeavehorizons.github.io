Welcome to Rosso Cadere!

This is a fan-run wiki dedicated to 8:11!

8:11 is an 18+ RPGmaker horror game made by indie dev Dubuk. The demo for the game was released in May of 2020, and the full release of Chapter 1 was released in July of 2021.
